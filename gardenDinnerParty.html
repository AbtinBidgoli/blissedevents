Garden Dinner Party

  • Posted By : Elizabeth Moradi
  • ~
  • Date & Time : May 10th 4:28 pm

Eight candlelit tables surrounding a large stone fountain were the focal point of this beautiful dinner party. Nude tablescapes allowed for the florals to really stand out and over 80 tapered candles lit the night sky as dinner was served.

My clients front yard is surrounded by lush greenery and it was the absolute perfect setting for ivories and golds. I chose white washed wood tables and ivory tufted chairs to go along with white and gold dinner and flatware. I always use several runners to give a full look under the florals and this time was no exception. Six ivory and champagne runners lined each table and shades of reds and creams in flowers sat in gold vessels on top. Four tables were styled with large centerpieces and the other four were lined with bud vases so as to create a different look.

Cocktails, a full table of appetizers and a sushi station awaited the guests before a full dinner was served later in the evening. This night was absolute dinner party goals!
