A Beary Cute Birthday!

  • Posted By : Elizabeth Moradi
  • ~
  • Date & Time : October 11th 10:37 am

Nothing short of a royal birthday for the Royal Twins (@theroyaltwins)! Teddy bears in all sizes adorned this beautiful birthday party at the girls home in Calabasas. Guests were greeted by a couple of cute bears holding rose gold balloons; and bear paws directed them to the backyard where they could play games, swim, enjoy some ice cream and so much more!

The color palette was the ever so popular nude and blush combination. Mauve, blushes and mocha balloon garlands accompanied the bears throughout the backyard and the showstopper was a 10 foot wide balloon rainbow!!

Guests enjoyed sweet and savory gourmet crepes, charcuterie and even personal pizzas!

The kids table entailed a rustic look with cream linens and wood crossback chairs. The table showcased two fluffy bears hugging nude florals as the centerpiece; while surrounded by bamboo bear plates, blush napkins and glasses, and of course mini honey favors for the guests.
